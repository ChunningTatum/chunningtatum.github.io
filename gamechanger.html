The Game Changer

Sleep is important, that is the time the body recovers. In order to function the body needs to rest, the required amount of sleep is 8.5 hours for adults. 6 Hours is considered the absolute minimum, but only few are aware of that. Sleep deprived people often find it hard to concentrate and have a higher chance of depression, heart diseases and obesity. A lot of people don’t get enough hours of sleep. Gerard Kerkhof, professor at the UvA conducted a research and found out that the people in the Netherlands sleep 7 hours at night on average. Also, one third of the population sleeps less than 6 hours. Besides, 33 per cent mentioned that they regularly (more than 3 times per week) are having trouble sleeping.

What if we could improve the sleep of your child and manage game time?

But what if your child doesn’t want to play anymore but your child also does not want to waste the time that is left. By simply pressing the head of the chicken the timer will stop and the remaining time will be saved. Your child is then able to use the saved up time on a different date. The Game Changer is a physical product, designed as a chicken. You as the parent can clock in how much time your child is allowed to play per session. After clocking in, the glasses will show how much game time is left. If the timer reaches closer to zero, the chicken will make some noise, indicating that play time is soon over. As the timer reaches zero, the electricity to the gaming console will be cut.
